# Follow-up Email Templates

Use these as starting points for generating follow-up emails. Adjust tone and content based on context.

## Template 1: Following up after application

Subject: Following up on {{ROLE}} application

Hi {{CONTACT_NAME}},

I wanted to follow up on my application for the {{ROLE}} position I submitted on {{APPLICATION_DATE}}. I'm particularly excited about {{SPECIFIC_DETAIL}}.

{{#RECENT_ACHIEVEMENT}}
Since applying, I've {{RECENT_ACHIEVEMENT}}.
{{/RECENT_ACHIEVEMENT}}

Is there any additional information I can provide at this stage?

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]

## Tone Guidelines

- **Conversational, not formal** - like you're writing to a colleague
- **Confident but not pushy** - you're offering value, not begging
- **Specific, not generic** - reference actual work, real details
- **Brief** - 3-4 short paragraphs max
- **Clear CTA** - what do you want them to do?

## Variables to fill

- {{COMPANY}} - Company name
- {{ROLE}} - Job title
- {{CONTACT_NAME}} - First name only (conversational)
- {{APPLICATION_DATE}} - Date you applied (e.g., "Jan 22")
- {{SPECIFIC_DETAIL}} - Something specific from the job description or company
- {{RECENT_ACHIEVEMENT}} - New project, blog post, skill shipped
- {{LAST_CONTACT_DATE}} - Last time you reached out
- {{DOMAIN}} - [your domain areas], etc.
- {{BACKGROUND_SUMMARY}} - One-line summary (e.g., "10 years experience in [industry], [key achievement]")
- {{PROJECT_EXAMPLE}} - Specific system you built
- {{COMPANY_SPECIFIC_HOOK}} - Why you care about THIS company
